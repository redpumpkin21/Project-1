About me

I grew up in a small city called Kannapolis, where I am eager to escape from. It's really fostered my desire to look for the unknown. It was one of the main reasons why I chose Archaeology as my first career. I travelled a lot and found really cool artefacts like almost made projectile points. Sadly, the hands on experience wasn't what I needed, so I switched to being a cook. Being a cook I could creat great tasting food with the ingrediants I had. The hands on experience was amazing, but the curiosity of the unknown was lacking more than I wanted. Then I found coding, it has the perfect amount of unknown and hands on that I crave for my life. No one will know everthing and it's constant learning and implementing what you learned. It's what I needed in my life.
