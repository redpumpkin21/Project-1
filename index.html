About me

I grew up in the small city of Kannapolis, NC. My desire to escape from small city life fostered my desire to look for the unknown. This was one of the main reasons I chose Archaeology as my first career, which involved extensive traveling. I found many interesting artifacts such as almost made projectile points. Sadly, the hands-on experience wasn't what I needed. I decided because of my love of cooking, I would work in a restaurant. While I enjoyed creating great tasting food, I discovered this path did not challenge me. The hands-on experience was amazing, but the curiosity of the unknown was lacking. Then I found coding, it has the perfect amount of unknown and hands on that I crave for my life. No one will know everything and it's the constant learning and implementing what you learn that excites me. It's what I needed in my life.
